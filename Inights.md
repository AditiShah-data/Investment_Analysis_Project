## Insights

- Financial institutions especially small institutions usually prefer those projects that are government owned.
- African Development Bank and African Development Fund are prominent fund investors.
- Most of the investment of private projects are invested by African Development Bank.
- Private projects need more investment than goverenment projects.
- Most goverenment and private projects usually focus on multinational projects.

## Conclusion & Recommendations

- Financial institutions usually prefer sovereign projects as they are government owned and trusted by many institutions.
- Government works for developmental purpose and profit is their secondary goal. Hence, this increases trustability on project for investers.
